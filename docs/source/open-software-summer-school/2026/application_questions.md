# 2026 OSSS application questions

These questions are provided so you can prepare your answers in advance of filling out the application form. 
Please be aware that we will only accept applications via [this form](https://forms.gle/S4jWFdQktkRxfHBo9).

## Questions
- What is your full legal name?
- What is your affiliation (e.g. university, company), including lab/department?
- What is your institutional (i.e. student/work) email?
- What is your job title or current degree program?
- What career stage do you most identify with?
  - Phase 1 - Junior (e.g. Undergraduate/Masters student, PhD candidate,  Research Assistant,  Junior Research Software Engineer)
  - Phase 2 - Early (e.g Research Associate/Fellow, Postdoctoral scientist, Research Software Engineer, Bioimage analyst)
  - Phase 3 - Mid (e.g. Lecturer, Reader, Senior Researcher, Senior Research Software Engineer, Research Group Leader)
  - Phase 4 - Established (e.g. Professor, Director of Research Computing, Distinguished Engineer, Chief Data Scientist)
- Are you affiliated with the Sainsbury Wellcome Centre or the Gatsby Computational Neuroscience Unit?
- Why would attending Neuroinformatics Open Software Summer School be beneficial to you and the wider scientific community? Approx 200–500 words.
- Describe your research and your research field. Approx 200–500 words.
- Which week 1 tracks (August 17–21) do you wish to attend?
  - Animals in Motion
  - Large Array Data
  - I do not wish to attend week 1
- Which week 2 tracks (August 24–28) do you wish to attend?
  - BrainGlobe
  - Extracellular Electrophysiology
  - I do not wish to attend week 2
- Do you have any accessibility requirements you would like us to be aware of? (if so, please specify)
- Do you have any dietary requirements you would like us to be aware of? (If so, please specify)
- Do you work in academia (or would fund yourself) or industry?
- Are you unable to attend without a fee waiver?
    - Please note, if you select "no" and are not selected for a fee waiver, you will not be offered an OSSS place.
- Please briefly explain why you require a fee waiver to attend.
- Do you want to apply for travel funding?
- If you require funding to attend, how much funding do you need (in GBP) and what will you use it for?
    - While the eventual costs may vary, please provide a breakdown of estimated costs in as much detail as possible.
